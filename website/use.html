Usage

Turn on your SFYL Wallet and press button on side for more than 5 seconds to show SSID and PASSWORD

SSID and PASSWORD are different any time you restart the wallet.

In this page you can see general information about the board and the name of the active wallet.

Change your wallets settings, load, save or delete wallet from the internal memory.

Copy the XPub and create a read only wallet on your Bitcoin node.

Generate an address using the information provided.

Create a PSBT in your Bitcoin node and paste in the form to sign it.

If you want you can also sign a textual message.
